  - Supported transmit only data without command.

## [2.0.4]

- Bug Fixes
  - Fixed MISRA 10.4 issues.

  - Fixed violations of the MISRA C-2012 rules 10.1, 10.3, 10.4, 10.6, 14.4, 17.7.
